    How to correct mistakes in ITR filing?

    1. Filing a Revised Return: A revised return is a way to correct errors in the original Income Tax Return (ITR) . It allows you to update or rectify any mistakes related to income, deductions, or other details. It is especially useful when you discover errors after filing the return or receive additional information that impacts your tax liability or require disclosure in your ITR.

    A.    What is the time limit for filing a revised ITR?

    As per Section 139(5) of the Income Tax Act, a revised return can be filed before three months prior to the end of the relevant assessment year or before the completion of the assessment, whichever is earlier.

    For instance, if the Income Tax Return relates to Financial Year 2022-2023, the corresponding Assessment Year would be 2023-24. Now, you have the opportunity to file a revised return until December 31, 2023, i.e, 3 months before March 31, 2024.

    Kindly note that a belated return, i.e., return filed after the due date can also be revised.
