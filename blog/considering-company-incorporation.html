    Why is it important to choose the right business structure?

    Choosing the right business structure isn’t just about ticking a box – it’s the foundation upon which your venture rests, impacting everything from personal liability to investor confidence. Opting for an optimal structure safeguards your personal assets, optimizes your tax burden, and unlocks efficient ways to raise capital for future growth.

    Whether you envision a small venture or a multi-million rupee enterprise, the right structure provides the legal framework and credibility to attract partners and inspire trust.

    • Shielding your personal assets: Some structures, like Limited Liability Companies(LLCs), create a wall between your company debts and your savings account so hiccups in business don’t disturb your personal wealth.

    • Taxing things up correctly: Different structures come with distinct tax advantages. Choosing the right one could mean keeping more of your hard-earned profits flowing back into your venture.

    • Opening doors to growth: Attract investors and partners with a professional structure like a corporation. It shows you’re serious about the climb and ready to scale new heights.

    • Keeping things clear and compliant: Each structure has its own set of legal and filing requirements. Choosing the right one simplifies paperwork and keeps you on the good side of the authorities.

    Pre-incorporation essentials

    Before your enterprise takes flight, specific steps are vital to ensure a smooth and successful departure. These pre-incorporation essentials are a must to create a strong foundation for your enterprise and your entrepreneurial journey.

    1. Choose the Right Structure for your Enterprise

    The first decision you have to make is to select the most suitable legal structure for your venture. Each offers distinct advantages and regulations, which we will explore later in this newsletter.

    Types of Company Under the Companies Act, 2013 are as follows:

    1. Private Limited Company (PLC)

    2. One Person Company (OPC)

    3. Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP)

    4. Public Limited Company

    5. Section 8 Company (NGO)

    2. Essential Documents for Registration

    1. Proof of Address and Identity: Valid documents of directors and shareholders.

    2. Digital Signature Certificate (DSC): Acts as your electronic signature for online filings and legal documents.

    3. We can file an application for DIN along with the SPICe+ for the interest of the directors from other entities.

    4. Nominee’s asset in case of OPC.

    5. Identity proof and residential proof of nominee in case of OPC.

    6. The Declaration from the proposed directors.

    7. DIR-2 consent to act as a director.

    3. The role of SPICe+

    SPICe+, which stands for Simplified Proforma for Incorporating Company electronically Plus, represents an enhanced version of the SPICe, designed to streamline the process of starting a business. This one-stop solution covers both company incorporation and related registrations, saving you time and effort. You can now say goodbye to juggling multiple forms.

    From now on, all new company registrations seamlessly occur through the online filing of SPICe+. It’s important to note that SPICe+ works hand-in-hand with AGILE-PRO, SPICe+AoA, SPICe+MoA and INC-9 – offering you a hassle-free startup experience.
