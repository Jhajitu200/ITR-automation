    2. Tax Holiday for Export-Oriented Units (EOUs):

    Export-oriented units engaged in manufacturing and trading activities can benefit from a tax holiday under Section 10A and 10B of the Income Tax Act. This incentive allows businesses to enjoy a tax exemption on profits generated from exports for a specified period, typically up to 15 years.üåê

    5. Research and Development (R&D) Deductions:

    6. Deduction for Investment in New Manufacturing Units:

    Under Section 35AD, businesses investing in new manufacturing facilities are eligible for a deduction of 100% of the capital expenditure incurred on eligible projects.
